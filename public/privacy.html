Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 19, 2026

Cocktail Cartography ("we", "our", or "the app") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting any data that may be collected through our website and Reddit integration tools.

Information We Collect

Website Usage

When you visit cocktail-cartography.com, we do not collect any personal information. The website operates entirely in your browser without tracking cookies or analytics.

Reddit Integration

If you use our Reddit recipe extraction tools, we may temporarily access:

  • Public Reddit Posts: Only public cocktail recipes and comments from subreddits like r/cocktails
  • OAuth Token: A temporary authorization token to access Reddit's API on your behalf
  • Username: Only if you explicitly authorize the app to access your Reddit account

How We Use Information

Any Reddit data accessed is used solely for:

  • Extracting and analyzing cocktail recipes from public posts
  • Building our cocktail database with proper attribution to original posters
  • Research purposes to understand cocktail trends and preferences

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage Only: The visualization uses your browser's local storage for preferences (like ingredient selections)
  • No Server Storage: We do not store personal data on our servers
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We never sell, trade, or transfer your information to third parties
  • Reddit Tokens: OAuth tokens are used only during active sessions and are not permanently stored

Reddit API Compliance

Our Reddit integration complies with Reddit's API Terms of Service and Responsible Builder Policy:

  • We only access publicly available content
  • We respect Reddit's rate limits and API guidelines
  • We provide proper attribution for content sourced from Reddit
  • We do not use Reddit data for training machine learning models without permission
  • Users can revoke OAuth access at any time through their Reddit account settings

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any data we may have about you (though we store none)
  • Request deletion of any data
  • Revoke Reddit OAuth permissions at any time
  • Opt out of any future data collection

Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies. The site may use essential technical cookies for basic functionality only.

Children's Privacy

This service is not intended for users under 21 years of age (legal drinking age). We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

For privacy concerns or questions about this policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.

Legal Basis

We process any data under the following legal bases:

  • Consent: You explicitly consent when authorizing Reddit access
  • Legitimate Interest: For research and educational purposes about cocktail culture
